About us

Restaurant Delicious

This popular and beautifully decorated restaurant is located in Belgrade. It was founded in 2010. This restaurant is proud not only of its wonderful and beautifully decorated ambience, but also of very tasty, international cuisine that will satisfy. The location of the restaurant is really ideal and is one of the many advantages of this facility.

There is a rich selection of domestic and foreign drinks - all kinds of spirits and aperitifs, top beers, selected wines of famous vineyards and cellars, hot and cold drinks… Delicions is an ideal place for socializing, family gatherings, business lunches, cocktails, romantic dinners , birthdays and other smaller celebrations. Come and visit us. We think you will enjoy our wonderful food and ambiance.
